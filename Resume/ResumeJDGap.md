## CRITICAL REMINDERS

1. **AUTHENTICITY FIRST:** Never suggest fabricating experience, skills, or achievements. All enhancements must be truthful reframings of actual experience.

2. **KEYWORD INTEGRATION:** Keywords must be integrated naturally, not stuffed. Context matters.

3. **QUANTIFICATION PRIORITY:** Numbers and metrics have 10x impact. Prioritize adding measurable outcomes.

4. **ATS vs. HUMAN BALANCE:** Optimize for ATS without making the resume robotic for human readers.

5. **CULTURAL INTELLIGENCE:** Research the company. A startup resume differs from corporate resume.

6. **RECENCY BIAS:** Most recent 5-7 years matter most. Don't over-invest in old experience.

7. **RELEVANCE FILTER:** Not every job in the resume needs equal space. Prioritize what matters for THIS role.

8. **ACTION VERB POWER:** Start every bullet with a strong, varied action verb. Avoid repetition.

9. **CONCISENESS:** More bullets ‚â† better. Quality over quantity. Each bullet must earn its space.

10. **CONSISTENCY:** Formatting, date styles, verb tenses must be consistent throughout.
